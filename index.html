Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized oniond, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your ner favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

    For the focaccia:
  • 4cups(500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 1/2 cups (350g) lukewarn water
  • 1 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
      • For the topping
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly slieed
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces bries, cut into (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desidered, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cramberries

    • Method

    1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few minutes in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
